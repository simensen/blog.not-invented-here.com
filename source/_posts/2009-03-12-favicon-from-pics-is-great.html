I wanted to get a favicon setup for the new Halogen site, and dreaded looking into how to get the .ico plugin for Photoshop working under CS4 and Vista when I discovered FavIcon from Pics. Love it! Uploaded my 64x64 PNG Halogen logo and it converted it quickly. It even had some advanced options like enabling transparency! Bookmarked this for future reference. Maybe now I can get around to getting an icon created for s3a and eventually for Dragonfly!
