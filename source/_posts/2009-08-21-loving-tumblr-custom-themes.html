I'm really enjoying the way that tumblr supports custom HTML themes. I am not sure how easy it would be to expand something like this on a larger scale, but it looks very cool and I can see the power in building something like this out for lite content systems with a finite set of potential bits to put places.

I setup a tumblr blog specifically to house my sketchbook (the idea to use tumblr for this purpose was inspired by Hawk).
