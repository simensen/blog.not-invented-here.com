I spent about an hour tonight looking at the new Facebook Pages revamp. I must say, I am a little disappointed by some things (the loss of fb:wide applications on the main page being top on that list) but one of the newer features has me so excited it may actually overshadow my disappointments: status updates! Since I jumped on the Facebook bandwagon last year, I had been pretty happy with my choice of Pages over Groups for my various pages (I have pages for s3a, Halogen and the Belltown Art Walk) with the exception with one big "but"... that being, how can I actually communicate with my fans? Send updates, you say? No good! After I sent a few I came to realize that nobody would ever even know I had sent them. I had to look long and hard before I could even locate them myself. Turns out they show up on a tab in your inbox, but do not even show up as a new message notification. As far as I can tell, there is literally no way to be notified that you've received an update from a page short of manually clicking Inbox / Updates... what a drag. Meanwhile, the people running the groups I had joined were happily sending off emails to everyone in the group while I was stuck shouting into a blackhole. Status updates may make all of the difference! Now if only I can figure out how to make 'em work... hoping that I'm not going to be seriously let down by this feature because I really want to try and get Facebook to work for me.
